My Pronouns

He/Him/His/Himself

Example: He is a software developer and his work is highly regarded in the industry. Himself is always striving to improve his skills.

They/Them/Their/Themself

Example: They are a graphic designer and their latest project was a huge success. They are constantly exploring new ideas and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

It/It/Its/Its/Itself

Example: I saw it at the store and asked it for its opinion. It said it didn't really care.
