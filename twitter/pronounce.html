About My Pronouns

Hey there! I'm excited to share more about my pronouns with you~

Pronouns: I use any pronouns, but I prefer neopronouns that are cat themed. Using my preferred pronouns helps me feel seen and respected for who I am.

Why Pronouns Matter: Pronouns are an important aspect of identity and can have a significant impact on one's sense of self. It's important to use someone's correct pronouns as a way of showing respect and affirming their identity.

Neopronouns: Neopronouns are a newer development in the realm of pronouns, and offer more options for individuals to express their gender identity. I find cat-themed neopronouns to be a fun and affirming way to express my identity.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my pronouns. Using the correct pronouns may seem small, but it can have a big impact on someone's sense of self and well-being.
